Our Services

Prototype Service

When you need to develop a new liquid cooling connector, coupling or valve, consider using us to build the prototype. We offer competitive rate and can usually deliver in less than half of the time other companies do.

Smart Factory

Our technology team has delivered complex projects for companies of billion dollar revenue in Laser and semiconductor chips, CNC leath, moulding, nano materials and other industries.

Process Perception

We use advanced machine visioning equipment to perform 100% online inspection of parts with lubrication coating. When the deviation is above the threshold, a cutter compensation signal is sent to the lathe machine. The system significantly reduce onsite technician work.
