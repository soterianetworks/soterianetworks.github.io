E-Factory

E-Factory is a suite of light-weight software services for manufacturing. It connects the data points from the factory floor all the way up to each level of management, streamlines the business decision, work assignment and quality control. It boosts the efficiency of your day-to-day operation, decreases costs caused by poor collaboration, improves the transparency of production management, and gives a true taste of smart manufacturing.
